Spanish Latte

Spaniards are famous for their love of strong coffee, and their version of the latte is as strong as you’d expect.

The Spanish latte is creamy, espresso-forward, and sweetened, though not as much as an American latte made with flavored syrup. You don’t have to fly to Spain to try this twist on a latte!

Hazelnut

This beverage combines espresso with steamed milk and hazelnut sauce, finished with a sprinkling of mocha powder.

The nutty creamy note blends well with coffee aroma.​ Available as hot, iced, or blended.

French Vanilla

French Vanilla flavored coffee is delicious, so if you are looking for a way to enhance your coffee drinking ritual then look no further. Our strongly aromatic French Vanilla flavored coffee will be sure to please. Rich in sweet vanilla flavors and heavenly fragrant, our natural flavorings will simulate the experience of having fresh vanilla pods seeped into your cup of fine roasted coffee.

Give yourself something to look forward to each day with this completely rich and full-bodied coffee. This French Vanilla flavored coffee will not disappoint!

Dirty Matcha

Dirty Matcha (more widely known as Matcha Espresso Fusion) serves up the perfect solution for those of you treading caffeine middle ground. Virgin tries of this beverage were met with nods of approval from us, so we recreated this drink without the coffee machine.

There are three main components to a Dirty Matcha: Matcha, milk and coffee. Lightly sweetened Matcha forms the base while milk allows a smooth transition in flavour between Matcha and roasted notes of coffee.

Caramel Macchiato

There are days when you need a sweet, warm cup of coffee. An espresso won’t cut it. But you get bored with the same old options – latte, cappuccino. Until a combo catches your eye – the mighty vanilla meets caramel in a cup of macchiato.

A cozy drink with a caramel drizzle that sticks to your soul.

Salted Caramel

Sweet and salty have always made a surprisingly tasty combination, and this coffee is no exception! Just one sip of Salted Caramel Flavored Coffee and you'll be hooked on this savory delight. It's great as a morning pick-me-up, or an after-dinner-treat.

White Chocolate

Our signature espresso meets white chocolate sauce and steamed milk, and then is finished off with sweetened whipped cream to create this supreme white chocolate delight.

Bon Cafe Signature

Our smooth signature Espresso Roast with rich flavor and caramelly sweetness is at the very heart of everything we do.

Matcha Latte

Matcha latte is a milk-based drink, and it works just like a regular latte. Except it has a shot of Matcha tea instead of a shot of espresso . By a shot of Matcha I mean a 2 oz serving of thick Matcha tea, sweetened and mixed into the milk foam as any other coffee latte would be.

Strawberry Latte

Served over ice, this strawberry latte offers stunning layers of espresso, milk, and homemade strawberry syrup. The strawberries are the perfect complement to bold espresso and velvety milk!

Nutella Latte

It’s a unique combination of espresso, whole milk, and Nutella. The Nutella latte has unique flavors that come with a regular latte but is sweetened with Nutella!
